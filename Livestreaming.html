What Is Live Streaming?

Livestreaming is a way to broadcast live video or audio content over the internet, allowing people to watch events as they unfold in real-time. It's like bringing TV to the digital world, where viewers can tune in from anywhere with an internet connection. It's used for everything from gaming and music performances to virtual events and educational sessions, offering an interactive experience for both creators and audiences alike.

How to Live Stream in a safe environment

Choose a Safe Platform

Use a trusted livestreaming platform with moderation tools.


Set Privacy Settings

Control who can view your streams and avoid sharing personal info.


Monitor Viewer Behavior

Be vigilant for harassment or threats and take action if needed.


Use Delayed Streaming

Implement a short delay to allow time for moderation.
