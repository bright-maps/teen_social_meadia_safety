Legislation & Guidance
Social media can be both enjoyable and risky for teens.
Guide teens to use social media safely by being mindful of their online activities.
Support safe social media use in teens with guidance and mindfulness.

How You Can Help

Open Communication

Maintain open and honest communication with your teen about their online activities. Encourage them to share their experiences and concerns with you.

Educate About Risks

Educate your teen about the potential risks of social media, including cyberbullying, online predators, identity theft, and exposure to inappropriate content.

Seek Help When Needed

If you're concerned about your teen's online behavior or safety, don't hesitate to seek help from school counselors, mental health professionals, or online safety organizations.

Encourage Offline Activities

Encourage your teen to participate in offline activities and hobbies to maintain a healthy balance between their online and offline lives. Encourage face-to-face interactions with friends and family members.
